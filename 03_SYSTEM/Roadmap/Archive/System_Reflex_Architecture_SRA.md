## 1. VISION
To transform V.A.N.T.i.S. from a system that the AI *manages* into a system that *manages itself*, with the AI acting as the high-level decision maker. By offloading deterministic logic to Hooks, we maximize token efficiency and system reliability.

---

## 2. PROPOSED REFLEXES (HOOK CANDIDATES)

### A. The "Sentinel" (Audit Log Formatter)
*   **Trigger**: End of every AI turn where a file was modified.
*   **Logic**: Accepts `agent`, `task`, `impact`, and `files` as arguments. Generates the `YYYY-MM-DD_HHMMSS_...` filename and the standard YAML frontmatter.
*   **Value**: Saves ~1,000 tokens per turn and ensures 100% audit compliance.

### B. The "Librarian" (ID & Metadata Generator)
*   **Trigger**: Creation of a new Galaxy note.
*   **Logic**: Normalizes titles into `concept-domain-name` format, checks for duplicates, and injects the `v1.0` metadata block automatically.
*   **Value**: Prevents "Naming Drift" and ensures every note is indexed correctly for the Knowledge Graph.

### C. The "Synthesizer" (Daily Narrative Assembler)
*   **Trigger**: Execution of the `/daily` command.
*   **Logic**: Scans the `logs/YYYY-MM-DD/` folder, parses the `Reasoning Summary` and `Outcome` fields, and aggregates them into a structured draft for human review.
*   **Value**: Reduces the AI's "Context Reading" load from 20+ files down to 1 structured JSON object.

### D. The "Janitor" (Galaxy Link Validator)
*   **Trigger**: Periodic maintenance or on-demand.
*   **Logic**: Scans `related_concepts` in all nodes. Flags broken links (`[[non-existent-note]]`) or orphan notes (0 incoming/outgoing links).
*   **Value**: Maintains Graph Integrity without the AI having to "browse" the folder manually.
