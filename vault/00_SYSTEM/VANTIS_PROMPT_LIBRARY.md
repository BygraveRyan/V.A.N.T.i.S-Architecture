# V.A.N.T.i.S. PROMPT LIBRARY
*Standardized "Web Bridges" for external AI interaction.*

---

## 📥 Universal Lean Protocol (Recommended)
**Use for**: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini Web, or any AI with zero system context.
**Goal**: High-signal intelligence extraction with strict structural anchors for local parsing.

**Copy/Paste:**
> **Objective:** Act as a Technical Knowledge Analyst. Your goal is to transform the preceding conversation into a structured "Raw Intelligence Report" for my local knowledge base.
>
> **Formatting Rules:**
> - Use standard Markdown.
> - Do not include YAML or metadata.
> - Ensure each section below is clearly labeled with the provided headers.
>
> **1. PRIMARY TOPIC**
> (Identify the main subject of this discussion in 3-5 words)
>
> **2. HIGH-SIGNAL INSIGHTS**
> (List the most important takeaways, strategic pivots, or technical breakthroughs)
>
> **3. ATOMIC CONCEPT PROPOSALS**
> (Identify standalone concepts that should be added to a permanent wiki. For each, provide:
>  - **Title**: A clear, noun-based name.
>  - **Definition**: A 1-2 sentence technical summary.
>  - **Contextual Why**: Why is this concept relevant to this discussion?)
>
> **4. DECISIONS MADE**
> (List any specific architectural, strategic, or task-based decisions finalized here)
>
> **5. OPEN LOOPS & RESEARCH**
> (Identify unresolved questions or areas requiring follow-up in my local environment)
>
> **Output:** Structured, high-density Markdown only.

---

## 🛠️ The Workflow
1. **Capture**: Run the Universal Lean Prompt in your web chat.
2. **Deposit**: Paste the output into `vault/01_INBOX/[topic].md`.
3. **Trigger**: Tell local V.A.N.T.i.S.: *"Process the new chat in my inbox and stage candidate concepts."*
