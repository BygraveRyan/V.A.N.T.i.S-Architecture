Description: Generates the Daily Master Audit log.

---

3.  **Audit**: Write to `02_MACHINE/Daily/YYYY-MM-DD_VANTIS_DAILY_MASTER_AUDIT.md`.
4.  **Confirm**: Echo "✅ Daily Master Audit generated."
